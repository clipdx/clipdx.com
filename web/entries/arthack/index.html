ARTHACK is a monthly evening of discussion for computer enthusiasts and artists. We'll be talking about and sharing work that explores the intersection of code, art, hardware, and society.

We're interested in talking about all kinds of work at this intersection. To help give you a sense of the breadth, here are things we're talking about:

  • New media art
  • Information art
  • Algorithmic art
  • Internet art
  • Digital poetry

We're inspired by groups like Wordhack NYC and the School for Poetic Computation, and are hoping to create a discussion space for artists doing neat work with computers here in Portland.

Come with a laptop and you can share some work on the open projector!

Time: July 14th, 6pm

Location: ADX (directions)
